Countdown in progress…

Arthur & Chase (and Thor, Max, and Murrby!) are thrilled to welcome a brand-new crewmate. Dax is already surrounded by so much love, and we cannot wait to share every new heartbeat, kick, and smile.

Milestones

  • August 2021
    Arthur bought a house in the mountains of Colorado, where he planed to raise a family.
  • July 2022
    Research into the surrogacy process started in earnest.
  • September 2022
    Arthur and Chase met. Chase was thrilled to be a co-parent.
  • December 2022
    Arthur had the initial consultation with Circle Surrogacy.
  • February 2023
    Arthur reconnected with Brit, a long-time online friendly acquaintance.
  • March 2023
    Arthur signed with Circle Surrogacy and got on the waitlist for a surrogate.
  • May 2023
    Arthur and Brit agreed to make embryos with their respective genetic material.
  • January 2024

  • November 2024
    Circle started presenting prospective surrogate profiles.
  • December 2024
    We matched with Jessie as our surrogate!
  • March 2025
    Herasight provided extensive genetic analysis and profiling of the embryos, helping with selection.
  • April 2025
    The Dax embryo was identified and transferred to Jessie!
  • May 2025
    Pregnancy confirmed!
