Dax Zey

In the Star Trek universe, the Dax symbiont lives many lifetimes, gathering experience, compassion, and a deep sense of duty. Jadzia Dax, joined with that symbiont, is brilliant, fearless, and endlessly kind. We hope our Dax grows up surrounded by those same values.

  • Curiosity: Jadzia is a scientist first, always ready to explore the unknown.
  • Loyalty: Her friendships anchor the crew of Deep Space Nine.
  • Balance: She embraces tradition while welcoming change—something we wish for Dax.
