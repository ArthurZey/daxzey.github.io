Our Surrogacy Story

Baby Dax's countdown started long before the third trimester. This is the map of how a Colorado dream, a team of brilliant guides, and a whole lot of heart brought us right to this moment.

Waypoints in Our Journey

2021

Finding Home Base

We purchased our mountain home in Colorado and carved out space for future bedtime stories, snow-day adventures, and visits from the people who would one day help us build a family.

2022

Choosing Our Guides

Circle Surrogacy became our North Star. Their team patiently explained every milestone so we could enter the process with steady expectations and big hope.

Early 2023

Signing On

Paperwork gave way to purpose as we officially signed with Circle, met coordinators, and started building the medical, legal, and emotional scaffolding that would hold us up over the coming years.

2023–2024

Connecting with Brit

We were matched with Brit, our generous egg donor, and quickly moved from formal introductions to real friendship. Group texts, photo swaps, and shared playlists turned coordination calls into genuine care.

Embryo Lab Work

Science & Safeguards

With Brit's help we created embryos, then leaned on Herasight's genetic screening to double-check that the tiny building blocks for Baby Dax were healthy and ready for the next leap.

Today

T-minus Weeks

Transfer, early ultrasounds, and weekly updates later, we're deep in gratitude for our gestational carrier, the professionals at Circle, and the family + friends cheering from orbit as we await Dax's arrival.

The People Behind the Magic

Surrogacy is more than timelines and test results. It's Brit sharing her heart, a gestational carrier tracking every nutrition note, coordinators who answer late-night questions, and grandparents who never miss a check-in call. Every person adds a strand to the web that keeps Dax safe.

We keep a "mission log" filled with thank-yous—meals dropped at the door, surprise care packages, uplifting texts before each appointment, and the countless reminders that Dax already has a village.

Lessons We'll Share with Dax

  • Home is intentional. We built it before we even knew him, because love is a decision.
  • Science is sacred. Circle Surrogacy, Herasight, and our doctors showed us that data and heart can work together.
  • Community matters. Brit, our carrier, and every helper proved that family is bigger than DNA.
  • Patience pays off. Years of paperwork, needles, and travel logs make his giggles even sweeter.
